# Round 2 Scope

Round 2 scope is maintained in
[`../../work/round-2/plan.md`](../../work/round-2/plan.md).

Round 2 cannot start until every Round 1 closure item is `PASS`, the owner
approvals named in that plan are recorded, and the remaining 0.2.0 release
blocks are resolved or explicitly exceptioned by the release owner:

- SGP owner acceptance of idempotency-key and envelope-version enforcement.
- eSocial regulatory owner decision for S-1030, S-1040, and S-1060.
- Release-owner decision for final `@esocial/contracts@1.1.0` publication or
  a recorded RC exception.
- Continued evidence that active XSD bindings use
  `packages/domain/src/xml/xsd/bundle/` and `packages/domain/src/sgp-lifted/`
  remains deleted.
