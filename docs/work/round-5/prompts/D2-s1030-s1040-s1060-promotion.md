## Why this is greenfield-internal (not owner-blocked)

- The XSDs (`evtTabCargo.xsd`, `evtTabFuncao.xsd`,
  `evtTabAmbiente.xsd`) are **publicly published** by gov.br; fetching
  them does not require external authorization.
- The S-1060 legacy-vs-current decision is a **product / architectural
  decision**, not an external one — it can be made internally.

## Tasks

1. **Resolve XSD source**:
   - Fetch the matching leiaute version from `docs/references/`
     corpus or pull the public schema; commit under
     `packages/domain/src/xml/xsd/<family>/`.
   - For S-1060: pick **either** retire-as-legacy (delete the
     `s2_05_00` golden, mark family as not-emitted) **or**

     this prompt files ADR 0014–0016).
2. **DTO** (concrete, replace `Round1Pending` for the family).

   `packages/contracts/schemas/v1/` and `examples/v1/requests/`.

   `packages/domain/src/builders/<family>/builder.ts`.
5. **Builder export** + dispatcher entry.
6. **Golden test** (byte-equal), **metadata test**, **invalid-DTO
   test**.
7. **Integration suite inclusion** in
   `tests/integration/soap-submission-pipeline.test.ts`.
8. **Lifted-source deletion check**: confirm no `sgp-lifted/` files
   for these families remain (R1 cleanup should already have removed
   them; verify).
